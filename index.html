Pez Solutions

About

Pez Solutions is a side-project that started as a way to utilize spare server capacity. The project is basically my homelab - a place to experiment with different technologies and services. It's now run as a prod-like environment, with a focus on stability and reliability.

Location

The servers are hosted in two locations: one in London (my bedroom) and one in Copenhagen. London is the primary location, and Copenhagen is the testing location. Each location has 3 servers divided into 3 different roles - A, B & C.
A is dedicated to monitoring and management, B is the primary server, and C is for anything that's not super critical.

Services

Pez Solutions offers access to a Plex server and a Jellyfin server. All media is legally obtained. Both services are available to friends and family.

Uptime & SLO

I aim to provide the best possible uptime, but please note that it is best efforts and not guaranteed.
I'm aiming for a Service Level Objective (SLO) of 99.9% (3 nines), which means that I expect the service to be available for 99.9% of the time.
However, this still allows for a small amount of downtime. Based on the SLO, you can expect a maximum of approximately 44 minutes of downtime per month.
