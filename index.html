Pez Solutions

About

Pez Solutions is a company that started as a side project to utilize spare server capacity. It has now grown to serve over 600 movies and 200 TV shows via Plex and Jellyfin.

Services

Pez Solutions offers access to a Plex server and a Jellyfin server. Both servers are available to anyone who requests access.

How to Access

To access the media servers, follow the instructions on the How to access page.

Uptime & SLO

Our service strives to provide the best possible uptime, but please note that it is best efforts and not guaranteed.
We aim for a Service Level Objective (SLO) of 99.99% (4 nines), which means that we expect the service to be available for 99.99% of the time.
However, this still allows for a small amount of downtime. Based on our SLO, you can expect a maximum of approximately 4.38 minutes of downtime per month.
