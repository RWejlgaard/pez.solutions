Welcome to Drive by Pez Solutions - your secure, private alternative to Dropbox and Google Drive. Built on the powerful Seafile platform, Pez Drive offers enterprise-grade file synchronization and sharing with complete control over your data.

How Pez Drive Works

Pez Drive provides a comprehensive file synchronization and sharing experience with:

  • Real-time sync - Keep your files synchronized across all your devices instantly
  • File versioning - Access previous versions of your files with complete revision history
  • Selective sync - Choose which folders to sync on each device to save space
  • Cross-platform support - Works on Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android
  • Web interface - Access and manage your files from any web browser
  • Collaboration tools - Share files and folders with fine-grained permissions

Pez Drive Libraries Explained

Pez Drive organizes your data into libraries - think of them as specialized folders with different capabilities:

Read-Write Libraries

  • Standard libraries where you can create, edit, and delete files freely
  • Perfect for everyday file storage and collaboration
  • Files are stored securely and are not accessible to administrators
  • Ideal for team projects, document sharing, and general file storage

Encrypted Libraries

  • Client-side encrypted libraries where your data is encrypted before leaving your device
  • Only you have the encryption key - even we cannot access your files
  • Perfect for sensitive documents, personal files, and confidential business data
  • Password-protected - you must remember your encryption password
  • Important: Lost encryption passwords cannot be recovered

How to Use Pez Drive

Once your account is ready, you'll need to download the appropriate client for your devices. We support all major platforms:

Choose Your Client

Visit the official download page and select the right client for your needs:

For Most Users - Desktop Syncing Clients:

  • Windows - Download "Client for Windows" (requires Windows 10 or above)
  • Mac - Download "Client for Mac" (requires macOS 11 or above)
  • Linux - Download "Client for Linux" (Ubuntu/Debian/Fedora)

These clients sync your files locally, allowing offline access and automatic synchronization.

For Network Drive Experience - Desktop Drive Clients:

  • Windows - Download "Drive Client for Windows"
  • Mac - Download "Drive Client for Mac"
  • Linux - Download "Drive Client for Linux"

Drive clients work like a network drive - access files on-demand without taking up local storage.

Mobile Access:

  • Android - Download from Google Play Store or F-Droid
  • iOS - Download from the App Store (search "Seafile")

Setup Instructions

After downloading your client:

  1. Install the client on your device
  2. Add account using the server details we provide
  3. Login with your credentials
  4. Create libraries and start syncing your files
